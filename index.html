The Laboratory for Brain Science and Medical Artificial Intelligence was established since 2019, affiliated to the School of Computer Science and Technology, Southwest University of Science and Technology, China. We aim to develp the solution, based on the machine learning, deep learning and signal processing for the engineering and clinical problems. We study the decoding algorithms for brain signals under different paradigms, and begin to work with the doctors from the several hospitals, such as The First Affiliated Hospital of Chongqing Medical University and Mianyang Central Hospital, to provide the auxiliary diagnosis for some diseases. Now, we have established long-term cooperative relationship with many well-known researchers and scientists, such as Prof. Dezhong Yao and Prof. Peng Xu from the School of Life Science and Technology, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China(UESTC), and Prof Cuntai GUAN from Centre for Brain-Computing Research, Nanyang Technological University(NTU), Singapore, etc. We are open to collaborate with the researchers to advance the development of research fields.
