The Laboratory for Brain Science and Medical Artificial Intelligence was established since 2019, lead by Dr. Yangsong ZHANG. Dr. ZHANG received the Ph.D. degree in signal and information processing from the School of Life Science and Technology, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China(UESTC), in 2013(Supervisor: Prof. Dezhong Yao). He received Master degree and Bachelor Degree both from Chengdu University of Technology(CDUT) in 2006 and 2009, respectively. From October 2016 to January 2019, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow at School of Life Science and Technology, UESTC(Supervisor: Prof. Dezhong Yao). From February 2019 to October 2019, he was a visiting scholar at Centre for Brain-Computing Research, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore(Supervisor: Prof Cuntai GUAN) He is currently an associate Professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Southwest University of Science and Technology, China.
